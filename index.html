About MarineEng Academy

What is this?

MarineEng Academy is a free, publicly accessible knowledge hub for aspiring marine engineers. The content is drawn from real training materials, work instructions, manuals, and field notes collected and organized by experienced Japanese marine engineers across multiple vessel types — LNG carriers, bulk carriers, and container ships.

Who is it for?

  • Cadets and junior engineers preparing for watchkeeping duties
  • 3AE/2AE/1AE candidates preparing for oral licensing examinations
  • Marine engineering students looking for practical, real-world reference
  • Experienced engineers looking to cross-reference procedures across vessel types

Content Note

Original source materials are primarily in Japanese. Each topic module on this site provides a full English summary and key topic index. The original files remain available as downloads for those who wish to study the source documents.

Materials cover NYK Line and MOL vessel operational standards but are broadly applicable to international merchant marine practice.

Disclaimer

This site is for educational purposes only. Always follow your vessel's SMS (Safety Management System), Flag State requirements, and current class society rules. Procedures may vary by vessel type, flag, and company. When in doubt, consult your Chief Engineer.
